VideoPipeline ("we", "our", or "us") operates an automated educational content creation and publishing platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information in connection with our application.

2. Information We Collect

Our application collects minimal information necessary for its operation. When you authorize our application to connect with your social media accounts, we may access your account identifier and posting permissions as authorized by you through the platform's OAuth process. 3. How We Use Information

We use the information solely for the purpose of publishing educational content to your authorized social media accounts. We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than the content publishing functionality you have authorized.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties. Your authorization tokens are stored locally on your own device and are not transmitted to any external servers controlled by us.

5. Data Storage and Security

All credentials and authorization tokens are stored locally on your device. We implement reasonable security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your information.

6. Data Retention

We retain authorization credentials only for as long as you actively use the application. You may revoke access at any time through the respective social media platform's settings.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to revoke the application's access to your social media accounts at any time. You may also request deletion of any data associated with your use of the application by contacting us.

8. Children's Privacy

Our application is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
